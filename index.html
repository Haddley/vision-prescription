Vision Prescription — Prism Finder

Estimates the prism prescription that lets you hold single vision, the way an eye doctor does: you look at a letter H and compare two views — “better with one… or two?” — while the app brackets toward your answer. Your only control: squeeze the trigger (or pinch) while the better-looking view is showing. Keep your head level the whole time — the exam pauses if you tilt.

This browser doesn't support WebXR VR sessions. Open this page in your headset's browser (e.g. the Quest browser).

This is a screening estimate, not a medical diagnosis. Share the result with your eye-care professional before using it.
